Here's a time line of Amelia Earhart's life:

  • Jul 24 1897 Amelia Earhart is born
  • Dec 28 1920 experiences her first flight with Frank Hawks
  • Jan 3 1921 takes first flying lesson
  • Jul 1921 buys her first plane
  • Oct 22 1922 sets altitude record for female pilots
  • Jun 17 1928 becomes first woman to fly across the Atlantic
  • Aug 1929 First Women's Air Derby, Powder Puff Derby
  • Feb 7 1931 marries George Putnam
  • May 20 1932 Becomes First Woman to Fly Solo across the Atlantic
  • Aug 24 1932 to Aug 25 1932 Becomes the First Woman to Fly Solo from Coast-to-Coast
  • 1935 first woman in Bendix Trophy Race
  • Jan 11 1935 first person to fly solo from Hawaii to California
  • Apr 19 1935 first person to fly solo from Los Angeles to Mexico City
  • Mar 17 1937 first attempt to fly around the world
  • Jun 1 1937 second attempt to fly around the world begins
  • Jul 2 1937 disappears near Howland Island
  • Jul 19 1937 Search for Amelia Earhart is called off
